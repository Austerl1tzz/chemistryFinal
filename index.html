Learn About Production of Nitric Acid

Hello, dear visitors! We are Abzhan Adel and Zhetpis Alikhan from 10E. Welcome to our website about the production of nitric acid! Here you will find everything that there is to know about it, from history to Ostwald Process. Enjoy!

The synthesis of nitric acid is crucial in resolving worldwide food security concerns. It forms a critical part in producing nitrogenous fertilisers, such as ammonium nitrate, and thus plays an important role in enhancing agricultural output. Nitrogenous fertilisers deliver big nutrition that promotes increased productivity in agricultural operations, and in such a manner, helps in supporting the growing population worldwide.

Why Study Nitric Acid?

  • Fertilizers.
    Agricultural productivity, feeding population.
  • Explosives.
    Used in production of TNT and Nitroglycerin.
  • Other Chemicals.
    Dyes, plastics, and other synthetic compounds.
